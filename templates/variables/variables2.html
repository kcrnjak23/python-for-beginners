Working with Variables

So, you’ve got your variable, and named it. You’re now free to use them to perform corresponding operations, just as you do with numbers and strings. Variables can be reassigned as many times as you want. Like this:

x = 7
print(x)
7
print(x + 3)
print(x)
10
x = hello
print(x + "!")
hello!
